Merge Data

merge1 <- merge(FIPS,county1,by="geoid") %>%
  group_by(state_name,year) %>%
  summarise(across(value,sum)) %>%
  ungroup() %>%
  group_by(year) %>%
  mutate(country = mean(value))

For all of my graphics I chose to use line graphs because they were the best choice for 2 numeric variables and the amount of data. To start things off I made a graph showing all the states over time with a average line. Most of the states showed a decline in the mid 1980s until the early 1990s. The numbers inlined steadily until just before the 2008 crash. I compared Kansas and Missouri because they are the two states I have lived in throughout my life and saw that they both have very similar line graphs in terms of trends. When I compared them by merging the graphs together, Missouri seemed to have larger numbers. However, I confirmed that the trends were the same.
